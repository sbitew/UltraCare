About Us

Nestled in the heart of Marysville, Washington, Ultra Care Adult Family Home offers a premium living experience for seniors at a surprisingly affordable price. This small-sized community prides itself on delivering quality care and comfort at a price that respects the financial constraints of its residents.

The neighborhood surrounding Ultra Care Adult Family Home offers an array of conveniences for its residents. The community is located just minutes from the Marysville Outlets, restaurants, casinos, easy access to I-5! , short distance from medical facilities. Residents also have easy access to a nearby Walgreens pharmacy, located only 0.1 miles away.

Within the community, residents can enjoy a variety of amenities designed to enhance their lifestyle and promote social engagement. These include walking paths, a beautiful garden, movie nights, and scheduled daily activities.

In conclusion, Ultra care Adult Family Home offers a perfect blend of affordable living, high-quality care, and a vibrant community in a convenient and comfortable environment.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple: Our home is managed by us, but belongs collectively to our residents. Our home is based on the belief that our residents are family. They will be treated with love, respect, and dignity. That’s why our residents and their families are comfortable and happy to call Ultra care AFH home.

Our services

  • Personalized care plans tailored to each resident’s needs
  • Medical Services, In-Home Memory Care, and Medication
  • Assistance with activities of daily living, such as bathing, dressing, and grooming
  • Nutritious home-cooked meals and snacks
  • Housekeeping and laundry services
  • 24-hour supervision
  • Social and recreational activities, including games, crafts, and outings

What We Don’t Do

By law, we are not allowed to provide the following:

  • Intravenous therapy
  • Sterile dressing change
  • Physically restrain or lock residents in
  • Give medications, prescribed or over the counter, that are not part of the treatment plan from the residents doctor unless in a written order

Dining Services

Our menu will offer a variety of dishes, taking into account the cultural and religious background and food habits of the residents. Menus are kept on file in the facility as served. All persons engaged in food service are knowledgeable and will observe personal hygiene and sanitary food service practices. All food is of good quality and is stored and prepared in a healthful attractive manner. Modified diets prescribed by a resident’s physician as a medical necessity shall be provided. No more than fifteen hours will elapse between dinner and breakfast the following morning. Meals will be served in the dining area to encourage socialization among residents. Tray service will be provided in case of temporary need. All meals will meet at least 1/3 of the servings recommended in the USDA Basic Food Group Plan – Daily Food Guide.
